How Sydney Businesses Can Turn AI Hype into a Practical 90‑Day Roadmap

This guide shows Sydney businesses how to turn AI hype into a concrete 90‑day roadmap. It starts with business outcomes, not technology, using workshops to uncover high‑value, repetitive workflows. A four‑factor framework (impact, feasibility, risk, data readiness) helps prioritise 3–5 practical use cases. The article explains designing human‑in‑the‑loop workflows with Claude, backed by robust data, privacy and governance safeguards. It recommends executing in tight 90‑day cycles with clear metrics, defined roles, and local expertise. Done well, AI shifts from one‑off experiments to a sustainable, Sydney‑first capability that compounds gains in speed, quality and customer experience.
